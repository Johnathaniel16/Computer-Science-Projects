Who We Are

Happy Rafters!

At Up A Creek we have adventures for all experience levels, even for newborns! We pride ourselves in being the best water rafting in the entire world.

Up A Creek is located in the beautiful hills of The Rocky Mountains, not far from the rivers. Nathan Velazquez started Up A Creek back in 1969 to share his love of mother nature and whitewater rafting. Up A Creek was his way of bringing this love to all.

History

Nathan has dedicated his life to this place and has built it with all his heart but unfortunately he passed away and gave the responsibility to Nathan Jr, who learned his love of rafting and The Rocky Mountains from his Father, We here at Up A Creek look forward to sharing this love with you through our tubing and whitewater rafting adventures.
